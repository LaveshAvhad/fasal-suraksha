Farming Best Practices

  • Use certified disease-free seeds.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I know if my crop is diseased?
A: Look for abnormal spots, yellowing, stunted growth, or wilting leaves.

Q2: Where can I buy recommended fungicides?
A: Local agricultural supply stores or online platforms.

Q3: Can I get support in local languages?
A: Yes, multilingual support is available through agricultural helplines.
